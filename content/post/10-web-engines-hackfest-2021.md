I gave a talk at the [2021 Web Engines Hackfest](https://webengineshackfest.org/2021/) on how SpiderMonkey compiles a `WebAssembly.Module`, covering compiler tier selection, streaming parallel compilation, background tier-up, lazy stubs, and code caching.

You can watch the recording [on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lnGj_3E2yBs) or check out the [slides](https://webengineshackfest.org/2021/slides/a-tour-of-compiling-a-webassembly-module-by-ryan-hunt.pdf).
